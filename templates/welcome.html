Totally Accurate Date Simulator

Welcome to the Totally Accurate Date Simulator! This is a totally accurate simulation of a modern "dating" situation:

Two attractive (to the extent that out-of-copyright images will allow) strangers, having a conversation in a mutually-agreeable location, over light refreshments.

When the date begins, you will be asked to select a series of actions from the list of usual date activities.

By judging your partner's responses to these actions, you will attempt to guide your date to a successful conclusion.

Romance awaits!
